RedWire is an online game engine for remixing and mashups.

RedWire games are made up of independent chips that are wired up to draw graphics, play sounds, and interact with the player. RedWire gives you powerful development features such as time-travel debugging and visualizing which chips are running. To make mashups, or to just save time, chips can be dragged and dropped from any game into any other.

Not only is RedWire an open source project, but all games on it are open source themselves. So go ahead and fork one!

To find out more, check out our tutorials and documentation.
